Right to Services Act

Accountability. Transparency. Timeliness.

What is RTS?

The Right to Services Act empowers citizens to demand public services within a stipulated time frame. It holds government officials accountable for delays and ensures a transparent redressal mechanism.

  • Timely Delivery: Every service has a fixed deadline.
  • Online Tracking: Check application status in real-time.
  • Penalty for Delay: Officers are liable for unexplained delays.
