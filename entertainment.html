Life in Thane

Explore Culture, Nature, and Recreation

Upvan Lake

The crown jewel of Thane. Perfect for morning walks, boating, and the annual Sanskruti Arts Festival. Surrounded by the Yeoor hills, it offers a serene escape.

Yeoor Hills

Located on the edge of Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Yeoor is a biodiversity hotspot. Ideal for trekking, bird watching, and experiencing nature within the city limits.
