About Thane Municipal Corporation

Serving the City of Lakes with Excellence

Who We Are

Thane Municipal Corporation (TMC) is the governing body of the city of Thane, responsible for the civic infrastructure and administration of the city.

Established to ensure planned development, the corporation oversees public health, sanitation, water supply, and urban planning. With a vision to make Thane a 'Smart City', TMC leverages technology to improve the quality of life for its citizens.

Our Mission

To provide efficient civic services, maintain transparent administration, promote economic growth, and ensure environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

Transform Thane into a sustainable, eco-friendly, and citizen-friendly city through innovative governance.
